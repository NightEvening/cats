Cats - all about it

Friends who never condemn

About

Cats are small fluffy creatures that act like they own everything they see. One moment they’re chillin’ like kings, next moment they’re running around like something broke in their brain.

They sleep a lot, judge everyone with their eyes, and randomly decide if you’re allowed to pet them or not. Basically, cats are tiny drama machines with paws and attitude. Even though they act like they don’t care, they secretly run your whole house. Probably.

Cats always support us in their own weird but comforting way, just sitting рядом like silent fluffy therapists who don’t say anything but somehow make everything feel okay.

They never judge us, no matter how weird, sad, or chaotic we are — cats just accept us like “yeah bro, you still cool.” They don’t judge us, even when we act cringe, fail, or just lie in bed all day — cats are basically the ultimate non-judgmental roommates with paws.
